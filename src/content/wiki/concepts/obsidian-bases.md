## Definition

Obsidian Bases are database-like views that work from notes and their properties. A Base can show files in table, list, card, or other supported layouts depending on the app and installed extensions.

## Why it matters for Notion cleanup

Notion databases often need a new home after migration. Bases are useful when the database view still helps you filter, review, or publish Markdown notes.

## Offpedia cleanup rule

Do not rebuild every Notion table. Convert only the fields and views that still serve a workflow after the content becomes local Markdown.
