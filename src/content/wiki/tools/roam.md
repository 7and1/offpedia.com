## One-line definition

Roam Research is one of the early tools that popularized backlinks and block-based networked notes.

## Best for

- Daily-note-driven thinking
- Block-level references
- Nonlinear research notes

## Offpedia perspective

Roam influenced many knowledge-base tools, but it is not local-first. Before choosing it, confirm that you are comfortable with a subscription, cloud dependency, and export-based portability.
