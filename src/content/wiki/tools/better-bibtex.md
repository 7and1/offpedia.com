## One-line definition

Better BibTeX is a Zotero plugin for cleaner citation-key and export behavior.

## Why it matters in Offpedia

Research stacks start to break when citation keys shift unpredictably or exports drift between drafts. Better BibTeX reduces that friction without asking the writer to move out of Zotero.
