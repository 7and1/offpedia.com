## Kit overview

Research Notes Vault is the starter structure for a source-heavy Obsidian plus Zotero workflow.

## Recommended use

- Keep literature notes factual and source-linked.
- Keep concept notes synthetic and reusable across projects.
- Create one project note per argument, paper, or report instead of mixing them all together.
